Heroes of the Duck Invasion

Civilian Heroes

Dr. Quackworth

A distinguished rubber duck with a Ph.D. in Hydraulic Engineering who first discovered how to navigate Bergamo's complex canal system. His groundbreaking "Float Theory" helped thousands of his fellow ducks reach previously inaccessible parts of the city.

Mama Mallard

Known for establishing the first duck nursery in the Contarini Fountain, she helped countless newly arrived rubber ducks find their place in the city. Her integration programs became the model for duck-human relations throughout Bergamo.

Artista Anatra

A visionary duck who organized the first Floating Art Festival, transforming the city's waterways into galleries. Her efforts helped bridge the cultural gap between ducks and humans through the universal language of art.

Military Heroes

Admiral Squeaks

Commander of the Northern Canal Fleet, Admiral Squeaks led the successful mission to clear debris from the city's water filtration system, preventing a potential crisis. His strategic genius earned him the Golden Feather medal.

Captain Waterproof

Leader of the elite Fountain Guard unit, she developed innovative floating formations that allowed ducks to create beautiful water displays during city festivals. Her techniques are still used in training new recruits.

Sergeant Splash

Head of the Rapid Response Squad, Sergeant Splash coordinated countless rescue operations during the heavy rains of 2023, helping both ducks and small toys reach safety. His motto, "No rubber left behind," became legendary throughout Bergamo.
